Frequently Asked Questions

What is an evaporative air cooler?

An evaporative air cooler cools the air by evaporating water, using significantly less energy than a traditional air conditioner.

Do evaporative coolers work in humid climates?

They’re most effective in dry environments; in high humidity, their cooling performance is reduced.

How often do I need to refill the water tank?

Depending on usage and fan speed, you’ll typically refill the tank every 6–10 hours.
